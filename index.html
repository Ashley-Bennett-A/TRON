Tron Javascript

A Tron clone made using p5.js as well as vanilla js and the usual HTML/CSS

Objective

Be the last player standing.
Use your light cycle to trap your opponent and try not to hit his trail or your own

Controls

Player One

W - Up

A- Left

S - Down

D - Right

Player Two

UP Arrow - Up

LEFT Arrow- Left

DOWN Arrow - Down

RIGHT Arrow - Right
